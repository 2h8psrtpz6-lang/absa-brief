Simplifying Data Integration — Eliminating Pipeline Maintenance Burden
Zero ETL → Engineering Capacity → Innovation Velocity
$1.24M / yr
The Problem
Data engineering teams spend an estimated 40–60% of their time not building new capability, but monitoring, fixing, and maintaining brittle ETL pipelines. Data movement between systems introduces latency, creates transformation errors, and generates additional egress cost at every hop. Each pipeline is a maintenance liability that grows more fragile as data volumes increase. Engineers who should be accelerating ABSA's AI strategy are instead servicing infrastructure plumbing — at $125/hr fully loaded, this is a quantifiable and recoverable cost.
The VAST Resolution
VAST eliminates ETL entirely. Telemetry is written directly to the VAST database via PyArrow batching — no format conversion, no data movement, no intermediate pipeline to maintain. By keeping all processing within VAST, the maintenance burden collapses. Engineering teams shift from pipeline custodians to builders of AI-powered data products. We quantify this at $1.24M in annual FTE savings from AIOps automation alone — with additional gains from pipeline decommissioning on top. At scale, 12,000 engineering hours per year are redirected from maintenance to higher-value delivery, improving agility, data control, auditing, scale, and availability simultaneously.
